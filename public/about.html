My Background

Fashion Design

Long story short. I study Fashion Design in Bogotá and found a passion for Women's underwear. I moved to Buenos Aires to deep my knowledge of how to create your own Brand. When I came back, I founded my own brand Paradiso di Beatrice which I managed for about 2 years. In 2015, I moved to Germany.

Administration

While living in Germany, I discovered new paths and careers in Fashion and got more and more interested in Entrepreneurship, founding, brand creation, project-/product management. To this point, I decided to study a Master in Fashion Management and Administration in Barcelona. I have in-depth knowledge of Business, Start-ups, Entrepreneurship and other relevant topics regarding Administration.

Web Development

As for many humans, Covid changed their lives. For me was an opportunity to discover and pursue new interests. I applied for the "Web Development course" in Cologne. Currently, I am working part-time as a Teacher Assistant. Simultaneously, I am expanding my knowledge by learning JavaScript, React, deeping my knowledge of CSS and HTML.

Social Engagement

In 2020, together with another 10 Spanish-speaking women, we founded the non-profit Organization Aliadas für Teilhabe und Integration. A Spanish-speaking organization that support women with migration background to successfully integrate in Germany. More specific, I have been supporting the organization with the corporate design creation of the logos, digital and printed artworks and the social media channels like Instagram.
